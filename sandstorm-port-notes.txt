This is a fork of https://gitlab.com/gitlab-org/gitlab-development-kit.git

You should be able type "make" and then "spk dev" and be good to go.


You may also wish to precompile assets, which should bring down the
size of an new grain from ~15MB to ~400KB. Do this in the gitlab/
directory:

$ RAILS_ENV=init ./bin/rake assets:precompile
